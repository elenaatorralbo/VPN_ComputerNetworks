Frequently Asked Questions

What is a VPN?

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) encrypts your internet connection and routes it through a server in a location of your choice, providing privacy, security, and the ability to access geo-restricted content.

Are VPNs legal?

VPNs are legal in most countries, but some have restrictions. Countries like China, Russia, and UAE have regulations limiting their use. Always check local laws before using a VPN while traveling.

Will a VPN slow down my internet?

VPNs typically cause some speed reduction due to encryption and routing, but premium VPNs minimize this impact. Connecting to nearby servers and using modern protocols like WireGuard can help maintain good speeds.

Can I use a VPN on all my devices?

Most VPN providers offer apps for Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, and Linux. Some allow router installations to protect all connected devices. Check the provider's simultaneous connection limit before subscribing.
